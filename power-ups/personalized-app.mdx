Athletes open **your** app, see **your** brand, not a generic platform. With a Personalized App, your business gets its own presence in the Apple App Store, fully branded with your name, logo, and colors.

<Info>
  **How it works**: CoachIQ builds and maintains the app for you. You provide your branding, and the app is published under your own Apple Developer account, so it's truly yours.
</Info>

## What is a Personalized App?

A Personalized App is a custom-branded version of CoachIQ published to the App Store under your business name. Your athletes download **your** app, not CoachIQ, and experience your brand from the moment they open it.

<Frame>
  ![Personalized App Example](/images/personalized-app-example.png)
</Frame>

**What's customized:**

- App name (your business name in the App Store)
- App icon (your logo)
- In-app colors and theming
- Splash screen
- App Store listing (description and screenshots, which we design with you)

**What stays the same:**

- All CoachIQ features (scheduling, payments, programs, messaging, and more)
- Automatic updates as CoachIQ releases new functionality
- Ongoing maintenance and support

<Note>
  **Currently available for iOS only.** Android personalized apps are not yet available.
</Note>

## What You Get

Your own App Store presence gives your business an edge that a generic platform can't match.

- **Instant credibility.** Parents and athletes trust businesses with their own dedicated app.
- **Everyday brand visibility.** Your icon lives on their home screen, keeping your brand in front of them daily.
- **A direct line to your athletes.** Push notifications reach them without competing with anyone else's messaging.
- **Higher engagement and retention.** Everything your athletes need is one tap away, which keeps them active and coming back.
- **A professional edge.** You stand out from competitors still running on a shared, generic platform.

## What's Included

CoachIQ manages everything behind the scenes so you can focus on coaching, not tech:

- **Automatic updates**: New CoachIQ features are pushed to your app automatically
- **Ongoing maintenance**: We handle technical updates and Apple requirements
- **Support**: Our team is here if any issues come up

## Migrating Existing Athletes

If you're already using CoachIQ, your existing data transfers seamlessly to your new Personalized App. Current athletes can switch to your branded app, and all future athletes will use it from day one.

## Common Questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Do I own the app?">
    Yes. The app is published under your own Apple Developer account, so it belongs to you.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What about Android?">
    Personalized Apps are currently iOS-only. We'll announce Android availability when it's ready.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I update my branding later?">
    Yes. If you rebrand or want to refresh your look, contact CoachIQ support to request an update. Minor changes (colors, text) can be pushed quickly. Major changes (app name, icon) require a new App Store review.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Do my current athletes need to do anything?">
    Existing athletes will need to download your new branded app. All their data and history will be there when they log in.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What if I cancel CoachIQ later?">
    The app is powered by CoachIQ's platform. If you cancel your CoachIQ subscription, the app would no longer function, but you retain ownership of the app listing in your Developer account.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
