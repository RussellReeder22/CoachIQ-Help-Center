<Update label="May 2026">

## People

**Block and Remove Athletes**

You can now block or remove athletes directly from their profile. Go to any athlete's profile and click **Block or Remove** in the left sidebar to choose between removing them from a group or blocking them from your entire organization. Blocked athletes are tracked under **Settings** → **Blocklist**, where you can manage or undo blocks at any time. You can also pre-emptively block someone by email before they join. [Learn more →](/people/block-and-remove-athletes)

---

## Inbox

**Unread Filter**

A new Unread filter in the [Inbox](/inbox/inbox-overview) lets you surface conversations with unread messages instantly — no more scanning the full list to find what needs a response.

---

## Website Builder

**Map Embed**

Add an interactive map to any page using the new Map section type. Great for facility pages, directions, or coaches with multiple locations.

**Per-Page SEO Controls**

You can now set a custom meta title and description on any individual website page, giving you full control over how each page appears in search results and when shared on social media. Find it in the page settings when editing any page.

**Hide Header Navigation Per Page**

You can now hide the header navigation on individual website pages. Useful for focused landing pages, sales pages, or any page where you want athletes to stay on task without nav distractions.

---

## Scheduling

**Skip or Edit Past Recurring Occurrences**

Coaches can now skip or modify individual past occurrences in a recurring booking series without affecting future sessions — useful for correcting historical records without disrupting upcoming bookings.

**Clearer Credit Messaging**

Athletes now see a clear explanation in the booking flow when they have credits but those credits aren't valid for the selected scheduler, reducing confusion and inbound questions around credit eligibility.

**Calendar Defaults to First Available Date**

The booking calendar now opens to the month containing your next available slot instead of the current month. Athletes spend less time clicking forward to find open times.

---

## Payments

**Subscription Length Controls**

You can now define exactly when a subscription ends, directly in the [product builder](/payments/product-builder#subscription-length). Choose from three options: ongoing with no end date (default), automatic cancellation after a set number of billing cycles, or cancellation on a specific calendar date. The initial purchase counts as the first billing cycle. Use billing cycle limits for finite training packages like a 12-week program billed monthly, or set a hard end date for season-based memberships.

**Payment Method Reliability**

Cards now sync accurately with Stripe, validation catches issues before they reach checkout, and default card selection works correctly throughout the payment flow.

**Checkout Matches Your Branding**

The checkout experience now respects your athlete portal's dark mode theme for a consistent look end-to-end.

**Active/Inactive Payment Method Labels**

The payment methods list now uses "Active" and "Inactive" labels instead of "Available," making card status immediately clear at a glance.

**Coupon Codes in Embedded Checkout**

Athletes can now enter coupon codes directly in the product payment pop-up and embedded checkout — no redirect to a separate page required. Promotions work seamlessly wherever you've embedded a product.

**Credits-First Checkout**

Athletes with applicable credits no longer see a credit card entry field at checkout. The booking flows straight to credit redemption, eliminating the common scenario where athletes accidentally pay with a card when credits were available.

## Scheduling

**Reschedule & Cancellation Controls**

Two new permission toggles in your [scheduler settings](/scheduling/scheduler-settings-limits-and-permissions) give you full control over athlete rescheduling:

- **Allow athletes to reschedule** — Enable or disable athlete-initiated rescheduling per scheduler. Useful for locking in camps, evaluations, or any session type where changes shouldn't be allowed after booking.
- **Block rescheduling within cancellation window** — Prevents athletes from rescheduling once they're inside the cancellation notice window, closing the loophole where a reschedule could bypass your cancellation policy and leave you with an unfillable slot.

---

## Payments

**Waiver Management & Compliance Tracking**

A brand new [Waivers tool](/payments/checkout-terms-waivers) gives you centralized control over liability waivers and terms:

- **Create and manage waivers** from Tools → Waivers with a label, URL, and active/archived status
- **Attach waivers to products** — require agreement at checkout before purchase
- **Attach waivers to forms** — add a waiver checkbox to any registration or intake form
- **Compliance Report** — see exactly who has accepted and who's missing, filter by status, and export to CSV for your records

No more guessing who signed what. Every acceptance is logged with the client, date, and method (purchase or form).

**Updated Billing Page**

The [billing page](/help-center/payments/update-payment-method) has a refreshed design. Coaches can now update their card on file, view all past invoices, and download receipts — all from Settings → Billing.

<Update label="February 2026">

## Athlete Portal

**Hidden Products for Paywalls**

You can now use [hidden products](/athlete-portal/hidden-products-paywall) to control paywall access without exposing every pricing option to athletes. Hidden products unlock paywalled pages just like visible ones, but they never appear on the athlete-facing purchase screen.

This opens up powerful use cases:

- **Grandfathered pricing** — Raise your rates without affecting existing subscribers. Hide the old-rate product so new athletes only see current pricing.
- **Invite-only access** — Restrict your app to a select group (travel teams, VIP athletes, tryout groups) without the product being publicly available.
- **Complimentary memberships** — Grant free or discounted access to staff, scholarship athletes, or partners without a visible $0 option.

Configure hidden products at App Builder → Settings → Pages Paywall → Hidden Products.

</Update>

## Automations: Major Upgrade

We shipped a significant update to [Automations](/automations/what-are-automations) this month, giving you more power to automate your business workflows.

### New Features

**Session Notes & Custom Fields in Automations**

You can now include session notes and athlete [custom fields](/people/adding-editing-clients#adding-custom-fields) (like skill level, position, or training focus) in your automated messages. Perfect for sending personalized session reminders with context about each athlete.

**Booking Notes**

Add notes to individual sessions or entire recurring booking series. Great for tracking athlete-specific information, session goals, or occurrence-specific details. Notes are visible from your [calendar](/scheduling/calendar-overview-and-navigation) and can be used in automations.

**Wait/Delay Actions**

Create timed sequences in your automations. For example: send a welcome email immediately, then follow up with a training tips message 3 days later. [See all available actions →](/automations/triggers-actions-reference#all-actions)

**Scheduled Check Trigger**

Run automations on a recurring schedule (daily, weekly, etc.) to check conditions across your athlete base. Use this to automatically tag athletes based on subscription status, send weekly check-ins, or manage credits. [Learn more about scheduled automations →](/automations/scheduled-automations)

- Fixed issues with email, SMS, and in-app message actions not sending correctly
- Resolved errors when adding tags to users via automation
- Fixed announcement sending errors
- Wait/delay duration can now be adjusted properly
- Trash icon now works correctly under wait/delay conditions
- Product list UI no longer overlaps in automation builder
- Scheduled check trigger now saves and fires correctly
