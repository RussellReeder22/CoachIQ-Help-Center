## What's NOT in All Activity

The feed focuses on **client actions** and **your interactions with them**. It does NOT show:

- Your internal notes about them
- Calendar changes you made (unless it's booking/canceling)
- Tag changes you made
- Email opens/clicks (Inbox handles that)

For comprehensive details, use the specific tabs (Credits, Transactions, etc.).

---

## Pro tips

**Spot engagement patterns**: Regular bookings every week + recent purchases = engaged client. No bookings in 30+ days = potential churn risk.

**Time stamps matter**: "2w 3d" means 2 weeks, 3 days ago. Use these to gauge recency without doing date math.

**"View Calendar" shortcut**: Don't navigate awayâ€”click "View Calendar â†’" on any booking to jump straight to that session in your Calendar.

**Empty feed?** If you see nothing, this is either a brand-new client with no activity yet, or filters are hiding everything. Click "All" to reset.
