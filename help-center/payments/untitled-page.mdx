## Update your payment method

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Billing Settings">
    From your dashboard, click your profile icon in the top right, then select **Settings** → **Billing**.

    <Frame>
      ![Billing Update](/images/billing-update.png)
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Update Payment Information">
    In the Card(s) on File section, click **Add Card**.

    Enter your new credit card details:

    - Card number
    - Expiration date
    - CVV
    - Billing ZIP code
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save Changes">
    Click **Add Card** to update your payment method.

    <Check>
      **Success!** Your new payment method will be used for future subscription charges.
    </Check>
  </Step>
  <Step title="New Step">
    
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Common questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="When will the new card be charged?">
    Your new payment method will be charged on your next billing date. Your current billing cycle is not affected.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What if my payment fails?">
    You'll receive an email notification if a payment fails. Update your payment method within 7 days to avoid service interruption.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I switch between credit card and bank account?">
    Currently, CoachIQ subscriptions support credit and debit cards only. 
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

<Warning>
  **Keep your payment current**: Failed payments may result in temporary loss of access to your CoachIQ account.
</Warning>
