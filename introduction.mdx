## Quick Answers

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How do I accept my first payment?" icon="circle-question">
    1. [Connect your Stripe account](/getting-started/connect-stripe-payments)
    2. [Create a product](/getting-started/create-first-product)
    3. [Add it to your scheduler or website](/payments/products)

    Athletes can now book and pay instantly. Funds appear in your bank in 2-3 days.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I import my existing athletes?" icon="circle-question">
    Yes\! Use our [bulk import tool](/how-to/import-athletes-spreadsheet) to upload athletes from Excel or CSV. You can import names, emails, phone numbers, and custom tags.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How long does setup take?" icon="circle-question">
    Most coaches complete basic setup in 30 minutes:

    - Payment setup: 5 minutes
    - First product: 3 minutes
    - Scheduler: 5 minutes
    - Website launch: 10 minutes
    - Athlete portal: 5 minutes
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Do athletes need to download an app?" icon="circle-question">
    No download required\! Athletes can access everything through their web browser. Optional iOS and Android apps are available for enhanced features like push notifications.
  </Accordion>
