## What is Microsoft Clarity?

Microsoft Clarity is a free tool that shows you exactly how visitors interact with your coaching website through session recordings and heatmaps. Watch real visitors navigate your site, see where they click, and identify what confuses them - all without slowing down your website.

## Get your Clarity tracking code

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create a Clarity account">
    1. Go to [clarity.microsoft.com](https://clarity.microsoft.com)
    2. Click **Sign up** (top right)
    3. Sign in with Microsoft, Google, or Facebook account
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set up your project">
    1. Click **New project**
    2. Enter your website name (e.g., "CoachIQ Website")
    3. Add your website URL (e.g., `yourcoaching.coachiq.io`)
    4. Select **Sports** as your category
    5. Click **Create**
  </Step>
  <Step title="Get your tracking code">
    1. Go to **Settings** (gear icon)
    2. Click **Setup**
    3. Select **Install tracking code manually**
    4. You'll see a script that starts with:

    https://www.clarity.ms/tag/XXXXXXXXX 5. Copy just the ID part after `/tag/` (usually 10 characters)

    <Tip>
      The ID looks like: **pg4r7pepzr** (random letters/numbers)
    </Tip>
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Add Clarity to CoachIQ

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Scripts & Analytics">
    1. Go to **Dashboard â†’ Website Builder**
    2. Click **Scripts & Analytics** in the left sidebar

    <Frame>
      ![Scriptsscreen Pn](/images/scriptsscreen.png)
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add Clarity script">
    1. In the **Scripts** section, click **Add Script Source**
    2. Paste your complete Clarity URL: https://www.clarity.ms/tag/XXXXXXXXX
    3. You'll see it added to the Scripts list
  </Step>
  <Step title="Publish your changes">
    Click the blue **Publish Scripts & Analytics** button at the bottom.

    <Check>
      **Success!** Clarity is now recording sessions. First recordings appear in 2-3 hours.
    </Check>
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What Clarity shows you

### Heatmaps

Visual maps showing where visitors click, scroll, and move their mouse:

- **Click maps**: See most/least clicked elements
- **Scroll maps**: Discover how far visitors scroll
- **Dead clicks**: Find broken or confusing elements

### Session recordings

Watch anonymous recordings of real visitor sessions:

- See exactly how users navigate
- Identify confusion points
- Understand why visitors leave
- Find broken features or links

### Insights dashboard

Automatic detection of issues:

- **Rage clicks**: Frustrated rapid clicking
- **Dead clicks**: Clicks on non-clickable elements
- **Excessive scrolling**: Users lost on page
- **Quick backs**: Visitors immediately leaving

<Warning>
  **Privacy note**: Clarity automatically masks sensitive information like passwords, credit cards, and personal data. Sessions are anonymous.
</Warning>

## How coaches use Clarity

### Find conversion killers:

- Watch where visitors abandon booking forms
- See if CTAs are being missed
- Identify confusing navigation
- Spot mobile usability issues

### Optimize for bookings:

1. Filter recordings to "didn't convert" sessions
2. Watch 10 recordings to find patterns
3. Fix the most common issues
4. Test and measure improvements

<Tip>
  **Weekly routine**: Every Monday, watch 5 session recordings of visitors who didn't book. You'll quickly spot what needs fixing.
</Tip>

## Common questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Is Clarity really free forever?">
    Yes, Microsoft Clarity is 100% free with no limits on sessions, websites, or features. There's no premium version - everything is free.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Does it slow down my website?">
    No, Clarity uses asynchronous loading and Microsoft's global CDN. It has virtually no impact on site speed.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can visitors see they're being recorded?">
    No, recordings are anonymous and invisible to visitors. Consider mentioning it in your privacy policy for transparency.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How long are recordings stored?">
    Clarity stores recordings for 30 days. You can delete them anytime or disable recording while keeping heatmaps active.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Best practices

### What to look for in recordings:

- **Booking abandonment**: Why visitors don't complete bookings
- **Form issues**: Fields causing confusion
- **Mobile problems**: Buttons too small or elements overlapping
- **Navigation confusion**: Visitors searching for information
- **Price sensitivity**: Quick exits after seeing pricing

### Heatmap insights:

- Elements with no clicks might be poorly designed
- Heavy clicking on non-links means visitors expect interactivity
- Short scroll depth suggests content isn't engaging
- Mobile vs desktop patterns often differ significantly

## Troubleshooting

| Issue                  | Solution                                         |
| ---------------------- | ------------------------------------------------ |
| No recordings showing  | Wait 2-3 hours after installation                |
| Clarity not detected   | Make sure you added the full URL with https://   |
| Can't see heatmaps     | Need at least 100 sessions for accurate heatmaps |
| Missing dashboard data | Clear your browser cache and refresh             |
