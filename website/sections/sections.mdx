## What are website sections?

Website sections are the customizable building blocks that make up the main content area of your CoachIQ website. They sit between your header and footer, allowing you to organize and present your coaching content exactly how you want it.

<Frame>
  ![Sections Hero Pn](/images/sections-hero.png)
</Frame>

<Info>
  Each section serves a specific purpose - from showcasing your training programs to collecting leads through contact forms. You can add, reorder, and customize sections to create the perfect layout for your sports coaching business.
</Info>

## Why section management matters

Proper section organization directly impacts your coaching website's effectiveness:

- **User Experience**: Well-structured sections guide visitors naturally toward booking sessions
- **Conversion Optimization**: Strategic placement of forms and CTAs increases lead generation
- **Professional Appearance**: Consistent styling across sections builds trust with potential clients
- **Mobile Performance**: Properly configured sections ensure your site looks great on all devices

## Getting started with sections

<Steps>
  <Step title="Plan your page structure">
    Before adding sections, sketch out your ideal page flow. Consider what action you want visitors to take first, second, and third.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add your core sections">
    Start with essential sections like Hero (introduction), About/Services, and Contact/Booking sections.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Customize and style">
    Use the backgrounds and styling options to match your coaching brand and create visual consistency.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add compelling CTAs">
    Place strategic buttons and call-to-actions throughout your sections to drive conversions.
  </Step>
</Steps>
