## Frequently asked questions

<AccordionGroup>
<Accordion title="What if I need changes after my coaching website launches?">
You can make updates anytime using the Website Builder interface. However, most coaches find they rarely need changes since we build everything comprehensively during the initial process, including [program integration](/content/overview) and [payment setup](/payments/stripe-setup).
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Can I see examples of other coaching websites you've built?">
Yes! During your initial consultation, we'll show you examples of websites we've built for coaches in basketball, soccer, tennis, and other sports at various business stages.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="What if I don't have professional coaching photos?">
We'll work with your existing photos and provide guidance on improving them. Many successful coaching websites start with phone photos of training sessions and improve over time.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="How quickly can my website start generating coaching leads?">
Your website is ready to capture leads immediately upon launch. We include contact forms, session booking integration, and [program enrollment](/content/programs) from day one.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Does my coaching website work on mobile devices?">
Absolutely. All CoachIQ websites are mobile-optimized since most parents and athletes browse on phones. We test every site on multiple device sizes before launch.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="What happens to my website if I cancel CoachIQ?">
Your website is hosted as part of your CoachIQ platform. We recommend downloading important content before cancellation, though we hope you'll love the integrated experience of website, scheduling, and payments working together.
</Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
