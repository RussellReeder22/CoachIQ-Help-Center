## **How to Use Number Blocks in CoachIQ's Web Builder**

<Frame>
  ![Numbered Steps Example Pn](/images/numbered-steps-example.png)
</Frame>

Number blocks are a powerful feature in CoachIQ's Web Builder, designed to help you break down processes, highlight training steps, or outline benefits in a clear and engaging format. This guide will walk you through the steps to add and customize number blocks to captivate your website visitors effectively.

### **Steps to Add and Customize Number Blocks**

1. **Access the Web Builder**
   - Log in to your CoachIQ account.
   - Open the Web Builder from your dashboard.
2. **Select the Number Block**
   - Click on “Add Section.”
   - Choose “Number Steps Section” from the available options.
3. **Add Your Content**
   - **Title**: Keep it simple and engaging (e.g., “Three Easy Steps to Get Started”).
   - **Step Headings**: Use clear, action-oriented phrases for each step.
   - **Descriptions**: Provide concise details that guide the user through your process.
4. **Customize the Design**
   - **Background & Colors**: Adjust to match your brand identity.
   - **Icons & Numbers**: Choose icons or customize the style of the numbers.
   - **Spacing & Alignment**: Ensure the block fits seamlessly within your page layout.
5. **Preview and Publish**
   - Check how your number block appears on different devices.
   - Save and publish your changes.

### **Tips and Best Practices**

- **Consistency**: Ensure that the style and tone of your number blocks match the rest of your website.
- **Clarity**: Use straightforward language and action-oriented headings.
- **Visual Appeal**: Select colors and icons that enhance readability and engagement.
