Answers to the most common general questions about CoachIQ — what it is, who it's for, and how it works.

## About CoachIQ

<AccordionGroup>

  <Accordion title="What is CoachIQ?" id="what-is-coachiq">
    CoachIQ is an all-in-one platform for sports coaches and training facilities. It handles your website, online booking, payments, client management, communication, content delivery, and athlete-facing app — all in one place. No more stitching together 5 different tools.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What sports and activities does CoachIQ support?" id="what-sports">
    CoachIQ works for any sport or athletic training discipline — baseball, basketball, soccer, football, golf, tennis, volleyball, swimming, track & field, hockey, lacrosse, martial arts, personal training, and more. If you coach athletes, CoachIQ is built for you.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How is CoachIQ different from Mindbody or generic booking software?" id="vs-competitors">
    Generic booking software (Mindbody, Acuity, Calendly) handles scheduling and maybe payments. CoachIQ is purpose-built for sports coaches and includes everything:

    - A full website builder (no need for Squarespace or Wix)
    - An athlete-facing portal and app
    - Credit-based scheduling designed for session packages
    - Video analysis and content delivery for training programs
    - SMS, in-app messaging, and email communication
    - Automations to reduce manual work

    You replace multiple tools with one platform built specifically for how coaching businesses operate.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Does CoachIQ work for individual private coaches or just facilities?" id="individual-vs-facility">
    Both. Solo coaches use CoachIQ to run their entire business — scheduling, payments, website, and client management. Multi-coach facilities use it to manage multiple coaches, shared calendars, centralized payments, and a unified Athlete Portal. The platform scales with you.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I use CoachIQ for my sports academy with multiple coaches?" id="multi-coach">
    Yes. Each coach gets their own availability schedule and can be assigned to specific schedulers. Admins see everything from one dashboard — all coaches, all bookings, all revenue. Athletes book with the specific coach they want.

    [Managing multiple coaches](/scheduling/managing-availability-for-multiple-coaches)
  </Accordion>

</AccordionGroup>

## Platform & Features

<AccordionGroup>

  <Accordion title="Can I build a website with CoachIQ?" id="website-builder">
    Yes. CoachIQ includes a full website builder with customizable templates, scheduling sections, pricing pages, testimonials, image galleries, and more. Connect your own domain or use a CoachIQ subdomain. No coding required.

    [Website builder overview](/website/overview)
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Is there a mobile app for my athletes?" id="mobile-app">
    Yes. Athletes access your Athlete Portal from any device — it works on phones, tablets, and desktops through the browser. For facilities that want a fully branded app in the App Store, CoachIQ offers a personalized app Power-Up.

    [Athlete Portal overview](/athlete-portal/understanding-your-athlete-portal)
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I deliver training programs and video content?" id="content-delivery">
    Yes. Create programs with lessons that include video, text, images, and drills. Optionally paywall content so athletes need a purchase or subscription to access it. Athletes view programs through the Athlete Portal.

    [Programs overview](/content/programs-overview)
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Does CoachIQ have automations?" id="automations">
    Yes. Automate session reminders, follow-up messages, welcome sequences, and more. Set triggers based on bookings, purchases, form submissions, or scheduled times. CoachIQ also supports webhooks and Zapier for custom integrations.

    [Automations overview](/automations/what-are-automations)
  </Accordion>

</AccordionGroup>
