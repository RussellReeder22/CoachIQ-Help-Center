## What you'll accomplish

Set up and organize your training programs, certifications, and resources so athletes can easily access content from their mobile app or web portal. You'll learn how to create sections, control access levels, and optimize the layout for maximum engagement.

## Before you begin

<Note>
  **Required**: At least one program must exist in Content ‚Üí Programs before you can display it in the Athlete Portal.
</Note>

## Understanding the Programs page

The Programs page displays training content, certifications, and educational resources that athletes access at their own pace.

![Athletesideprograms Pn](/images/athletesideprograms.png)

### Programs vs Sessions distinction

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Quick Reference">
    | Feature | Programs                 | Sessions               |
    | ------- | ------------------------ | ---------------------- |
    | Type    | Self-paced content       | Scheduled bookings     |
    | Shows   | Progress percentage      | Available time slots   |
    | Payment | One-time or subscription | Per session or credits |
    | Access  | Immediate after purchase | At scheduled time      |
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="When to Use">
    **Use Programs for:**

    - Self-paced training content
    - Video tutorials and lessons
    - Certification courses
    - Educational resources

    **Use Sessions for:**

    - Scheduled training times
    - Live coaching sessions
    - Bookable appointments
    - Group classes
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

### Athlete view components

- Program thumbnails with visual previews
- Lesson and media counts (format: "X lessons ‚Ä¢ Y media")
- Completion progress for started programs
- Section-based organization

## Activating the Programs page

Control athlete visibility of the Programs section through the Active toggle.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Locate the Active toggle">
    Find the **Active** toggle in the top right corner of the Programs page.

    ![Coachiq App Builder Page Actions Menu Pn](/images/coachiq-app-builder-page-actions-menu.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Toggle visibility">
    - **ON**: Programs visible to all athletes in their portal
    - **OFF**: Programs completely hidden from athlete view

    <Warning>
      Deactivating hides all programs immediately, including those athletes are currently completing. Verify this is your intended action before proceeding.
    </Warning>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Update the page">
    Click **Update Page** to save your changes.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Adding individual programs

<Steps>
  <Step title="Click Add Content">
    In any section, click the **\+ Add Content** button.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select programs">
    The selection modal displays:

    - Thumbnail image
    - Program title
    - Lesson and media count

    Select programs to add to this section. You can select multiple programs at once.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm selection">
    Click **Save** to add selected programs.

    <Check>
      Programs appear immediately with thumbnails and details visible.
    </Check>
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Creating and managing sections

Sections organize programs into logical categories for easier navigation.

### Adding a new section

![Coachiq App Builder Add Section Button Pn](/images/coachiq-app-builder-add-section-button.png)

<Steps>
  <Step title="Click Add Section">
    Click **\+ Add Section** at the bottom of your program list.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Name your section">
    Enter a descriptive name. Examples:

    - "Education"
    - "Triple Threat"
    - "Follow Along Workouts"
    - "Beginner Fundamentals"
    - "Elite Training"
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start adding programs">
    Use **\+ Add Content** within the new section to add programs.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Setting up a Welcome Section

The Welcome Section prominently displays one featured program at the top of your Programs page.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Locate Welcome Section">
    Find the **Welcome Section** field at the top of Programs configuration.

    ![Coachiq App Builder Welcome Section Dropdown Pn](/images/coachiq-app-builder-welcome-section-dropdown.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select featured section">
    Choose which section becomes the welcome section. The first program in this section receives featured treatment.

    <Tip>
      **Effective Welcome Section uses:**

      - Getting-started program for new athletes
      - Signature certification course
      - Limited-time training series
      - Most popular program
    </Tip>
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Program access and sharing settings

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open program settings">
    Click **Share** on any program.

    ![Coachiq Program Builder Share Button Pn](/images/coachiq-program-builder-share-button.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose access type">
    <Tabs>
      <Tab title="Free">
        **Free Access**

        - Open to all athletes
        - No payment required
        - Great for introductory content
        - Builds trust and engagement
      </Tab>
      <Tab title="Paywalled">
        **Paywalled Access**

        - Requires purchase or membership
        - Generates revenue
        - Premium content protection
        - Supports subscriptions
      </Tab>
      <Tab title="Private">
        **Private Access**

        - Manual assignment only
        - Exclusive content
        - Personalized programs
        - Beta testing new content
      </Tab>
    </Tabs>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure private access">
    For Private programs, toggle ON to restrict visibility to assigned athletes only.

    ![Coachiq Program Paywall Private Settings Pn](/images/coachiq-program-paywall-private-settings.png)

    **Use cases for private programs:**

    - Elite athlete exclusive content
    - Beta testing new programs
    - Personalized training plans
    - Team-specific content
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save settings">
    Click **Save** to apply access configuration.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Common questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Where do I create new programs?">
    Programs are created in **Content ‚Üí Programs** in your main admin dashboard. The App Builder only controls display configuration.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Why can't athletes see certain programs?">
    Diagnostic steps:

    1. **Programs page Active toggle** ‚Üí Must be ON
    2. **Program access setting** ‚Üí Check if Private or Paywalled
    3. **Athlete status** ‚Üí Verify purchase or assignment
    4. **Platform** ‚Üí iOS/Android have purchase restrictions
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can athletes search or filter programs?">
    No search/filter capability currently exists. Structure your sections thoughtfully for easy navigation.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How does progress tracking work?">

## Troubleshooting

### Programs not appearing in selection modal

- **Verify**: Programs exist in Content ‚Üí Programs
- **Confirm**: Programs are published (not draft)
- **Action**: Refresh App Builder page

### Athletes report missing programs

1. **Check**: Programs page Active toggle ‚Üí Must be ON
2. **Check**: Program access type ‚Üí Not Private without assignment
3. **Check**: Payment status ‚Üí Purchase complete for paywalled
4. **Check**: Platform restrictions ‚Üí iOS/Android restrict in-app purchases

### Thumbnails not displaying correctly

- Thumbnails configured in Program Builder only
- Cannot override in App Builder
- Verify proper aspect ratio in source

### Section organization issues

- Confirm drag handle engagement
- Test in different browser if needed
- Always Update Page after reorganizing
