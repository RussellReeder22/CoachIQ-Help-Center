<Info>
**Required for**: Coaches transferring from MindBody to CoachIQ
</Info>

To migrate your clients, subscriptions, and scheduling data from MindBody to CoachIQ, our team needs temporary staff access to your MindBody account. This is a one-time setup that takes about 5 minutes.

## Before you begin

<Note>
**You'll need:**

- Owner or Manager access to your MindBody account
- The CoachIQ transfer email: **transfers@coachiq.io**
</Note>

## Add CoachIQ as a staff member

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open your MindBody staff page">
    Log in to your MindBody account and navigate to the **Staff** section from the main menu.

    Depending on your MindBody version, this may be under **Settings → Staff** or accessible directly from the side navigation.

    <Frame>
      ![MindBody help article showing how to navigate to the staff page](/images/mindbody-add-staff.png)
    </Frame>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Click Add Staff Member">
    Click the **Add Staff Member** button to create a new staff profile.

    Enter the following details:

    - **First Name**: CoachIQ
    - **Last Name**: Transfers
    - **Email**: transfers@coachiq.io

    <Info>
    Using a shared transfer email means our team can pick up your migration immediately — no waiting on a specific person.
    </Info>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Set permissions">
    Assign the staff profile a permission level that allows read access to your account data. A **Manager** permission group works best.

    CoachIQ needs access to:

    - **Client list and contact information**
    - **Memberships and contracts** (subscriptions)
    - **Payment and billing history**
    - **Class and appointment schedules**
    - **Reports** (for data export)

    <Frame>
      ![MindBody staff permissions settings showing available access levels](/images/mindbody-staff-permissions.png)
    </Frame>

    <Tip>
    If you don't want to use the default Manager group, you can create a custom permission group with read-only access to the areas listed above. See MindBody's guide on [creating permission groups](https://support.mindbodyonline.com/s/article/203253763-Permission-groups?language=en_US).
    </Tip>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Save and notify CoachIQ">
    Click **Save** to create the staff profile.

    Then let your CoachIQ account manager know that access has been granted — either through your existing chat or by emailing [info@coachiq.io](mailto:info@coachiq.io).

    <Check>
    **Done!** CoachIQ will log in and begin reviewing your data for migration, usually within 1 business day.
    </Check>
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What CoachIQ does with this access

Once we're in your MindBody account, our team will:

1. **Review your client list** — names, emails, phone numbers, and any custom fields
2. **Document memberships and contracts** — active subscriptions, pricing, billing schedules
3. **Export class and appointment history** — so we can recreate your schedulers in CoachIQ
4. **Map your data to CoachIQ** — match MindBody products to CoachIQ equivalents

<Info>
We only read your data — we will never modify, delete, or change anything in your MindBody account.
</Info>

## Common questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What permission level should I give CoachIQ?">
    **Manager** is the easiest option — it gives broad read access without giving full owner control.

    If you'd prefer more restrictive access, create a custom permission group with access to:
    - Clients (view)
    - Reports (view)
    - Appointments/classes (view)
    - Memberships/contracts (view)

    CoachIQ does **not** need access to modify settings, process payments, or manage your staff.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I remove CoachIQ's access after the transfer?">
    Yes — and you should. Once your migration to CoachIQ is complete, simply go back to **Staff**, find the CoachIQ Transfers profile, and deactivate or delete it.

    Your CoachIQ account manager will let you know when the transfer is complete and it's safe to remove access.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Will this affect my current MindBody operations?">
    No. Adding a staff member doesn't change anything about how your MindBody account works. Your clients, schedules, and billing continue as normal. CoachIQ's access is read-only — we're just looking at your data to plan the migration.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What if I don't have owner access to MindBody?">
    Only owners and some manager-level accounts can add staff members. If you don't have the right permissions:

    1. Ask the account owner to add CoachIQ using the steps above
    2. Or contact MindBody support to upgrade your access level

    You can also [book an Office Hours call](https://cal.com/team/coach-iq/office-hours) and we'll walk through it together.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="I use a different platform — not MindBody">
    No problem. Reach out to your CoachIQ account manager or email [info@coachiq.io](mailto:info@coachiq.io) and we'll provide specific instructions for your platform.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## What happens next

After you grant access, your CoachIQ team will:

1. **Log in and audit your data** — usually within 1 business day
2. **Create a migration plan** — map your MindBody setup to CoachIQ
3. **Import your clients** — transfer contacts, tags, and relevant history
4. **Rebuild your schedule** — recreate your services as CoachIQ schedulers
5. **Set up subscriptions** — connect recurring billing through Stripe
6. **Go live** — confirm everything looks right and you're ready to switch

<Tip>
The smoother the access setup, the faster we can get your transfer done. Most migrations complete within a few days of receiving access.
</Tip>
