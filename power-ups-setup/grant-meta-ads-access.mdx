Before CoachIQ can manage your Facebook and Instagram ad campaigns, you need to grant access to your Meta Business Manager. This is a one-time setup that takes about 5 minutes.

## Before you begin

<Note>
**You'll need:**

- Admin access to your [Meta Business Manager](https://business.facebook.com)
- An ad account connected to your Business Manager
- A Facebook Page connected to your Business Manager
- The email address provided by your CoachIQ account manager
</Note>

<Warning>
You must have **Admin permissions** on your Business Manager to grant access. If you're not sure whether you're an Admin, check with whoever originally set up your Meta account — or reach out to CoachIQ and we'll help you figure it out.
</Warning>

## Grant access

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Business Settings">
    Go to [business.facebook.com](https://business.facebook.com) and log in with your Facebook account.

    Click the **Settings** gear icon in the bottom-left corner to open **Business Settings**.

    <Tip>
    Bookmark this page — you'll come back here anytime you need to manage permissions.
    </Tip>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Add your CoachIQ account manager">
    In the left sidebar under **Users**, click **People**.

    Click the **Add** button in the top-right corner, then:

    1. Enter the email address your CoachIQ account manager provided
    2. Set the role to **Employee**
    3. Click **Next**

    <Info>
    **Why Employee?** Employee access is all CoachIQ needs to manage your campaigns. You keep full Admin control of your Business Manager.
    </Info>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Assign ad account access">
    After adding the person, you'll be prompted to assign assets.

    1. Click **Ad Accounts** in the asset list
    2. Select the ad account you want CoachIQ to manage
    3. Toggle on **Admin** access
    4. Click **Save Changes**

    <Info>
    **Why Admin on the ad account?** This allows CoachIQ to create campaigns, adjust budgets, manage audiences, and access performance data — everything needed to run your ads effectively.
    </Info>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Assign Facebook Page access">
    Still in the asset assignment screen:

    1. Click **Pages** in the asset list
    2. Select your Facebook Page
    3. Toggle on **Create content**, **Manage ads**, and **View performance**
    4. Click **Save Changes**
  </Step>

  <Step title="Confirm the invitation was sent">
    Your CoachIQ account manager now appears under **People** with a **Pending** status.

    They'll accept the invitation within 1 business day, and the status will change to **Active**.

    <Check>
    **Done!** CoachIQ will confirm directly with you once access is accepted and campaign setup can begin.
    </Check>
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Understanding access levels

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What can CoachIQ do with this access?">
    With Employee + Ad Account Admin access, CoachIQ can:

    - Create and manage ad campaigns
    - Adjust budgets and bidding strategies
    - Create and edit audiences
    - Access performance reports and analytics
    - Publish ads from your Facebook Page

    **What CoachIQ cannot do:**

    - Delete your ad account
    - Change account ownership
    - Access your personal Facebook profile
    - Remove other team members from your Business Manager
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I revoke access later?">
    Yes — you maintain full ownership and can revoke access at any time:

    1. Go to [Meta Business Settings](https://business.facebook.com/settings)
    2. Click **People** under Users
    3. Find the CoachIQ team member
    4. Click **Remove**

    Your ad campaigns and data remain in your account even after removing access.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Will CoachIQ see my personal information?">
    No. CoachIQ only has access to the business assets you explicitly assigned (ad account and Page). They cannot see personal messages, photos, or profile information.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="I don't see the People section in my Business Manager">
    You likely don't have Admin permissions on the Business Manager itself.

    **What to do:** Contact whoever originally set up your Meta account and ask them to either upgrade you to Admin or add CoachIQ directly. You can also reach out to us and we'll walk you through it.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="The email address isn't being accepted">
    A few things to check:

    - Verify the email with your CoachIQ account manager (watch for typos)
    - Make sure you're entering it in the **People → Add** field, not the "Add Partner" field
    - The email may already be associated with another Business Manager

    If it still doesn't work, ask your CoachIQ account manager for the **Business Manager ID** and use the partner method below.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="I have multiple ad accounts — which one do I assign?">
    Assign the ad account you want CoachIQ to manage for your coaching business. If you're not sure which one, your account manager will confirm during your discovery call.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="I don't have a Business Manager — just a personal ad account">
    You'll need to create a Business Manager first. Meta requires it for granting ad account access to partners.

    Follow Meta's guide to [create a Business Manager account](https://www.facebook.com/business/help/1710077379203657), then come back here to grant access.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="I'm getting a 'You've reached the limit for people' error">
    Meta limits the number of people with access to each ad account.

    **Fix:** Remove inactive team members or previous agency access under **People**, then try adding CoachIQ again. If you're still stuck, contact Meta Business Support.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="CoachIQ hasn't accepted the invitation">
    Invitations are typically accepted within 1 business day. If it's been longer:

    1. Verify you used the correct email address
    2. Check that the status shows **Pending** under People
    3. Message your CoachIQ account manager to confirm

    If needed, screenshot your Business Settings showing the pending status and send it to your account manager.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Alternative: Grant access using Business Manager ID

If the email method isn't working, you can grant access using CoachIQ's Business Manager ID instead.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Get the Business Manager ID">
    Ask your CoachIQ account manager for the Business Manager ID (a string of numbers).
  </Step>

  <Step title="Add as a partner">
    In Business Settings:

    1. Go to **Ad Accounts** in the left sidebar
    2. Select your ad account
    3. Click **Add Partner**
    4. Enter the Business Manager ID
    5. Assign **Admin** access
    6. Click **Save Changes**

    <Note>
    The partner method grants access at the ad account level. Your account manager may ask you to also grant Page access separately under **Pages → Add Partner**.
    </Note>
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What happens next

After you grant access, your CoachIQ account manager will:

1. **Accept the invitation** — usually within 1 business day
2. **Audit your current setup** — review any existing campaigns, audiences, and pixel data
3. **Schedule campaign planning** — discuss your goals, target audience, and budget
4. **Launch your campaigns** — based on your approved strategy
5. **Send weekly reports** — so you always know how your ads are performing

<Tip>
Schedule a quick call with your account manager right after granting access to get the ball rolling on your ad strategy.
</Tip>
